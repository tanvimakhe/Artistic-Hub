Fireside star

Rs. 3500

Category: Landscape Painting

"'Fireside Star' portrays a cozy scene in a cold forest where a couple finds warmth and comfort by a crackling fire. A tent stands nearby, offering a sheltered retreat amidst the wintry landscape. As they gaze into the starry night, a shooting star streaks across the sky, adding a touch of magic to the moment. The painting captures the intimate joy of shared warmth, nature's beauty, and the enchantment of a shooting star in the tranquil wilderness."
Canvas size 12*12 (30*30 cm)
