Winter's Sunset Glow

Rs. 3000

Category: Landscape Painting

In "Winter's Sunset Glow," nature transforms into a magical spectacle as the sun sets over a snowy forest. The pristine snow-covered landscape is bathed in warm hues of orange and pink, creating a serene and enchanting atmosphere. The skeletal branches of trees stand in stark contrast against the wintry backdrop, casting long shadows. The painting captures the quiet beauty of a winter evening, where the sun's last rays illuminate the snowy expanse, turning the forest into a tranquil haven of peace and stillness.
Canvas Size 12*16 (30*40cm)
