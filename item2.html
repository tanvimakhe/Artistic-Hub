Azure Elegance

Rs.2500

Category: Acrylic Painting

"Azure Elegance" is a captivating painting that showcases a blue and white flower, delicately cradled in a warm brown pot. The serene blue petals of the flower contrast beautifully against the purity of white, while the earthy tones of the pot provide a grounded and rustic backdrop. The composition captures the harmony between the vivid floral hues and the subtle richness of the pot. Each brushstroke reflects the play of light and shadow, emphasizing the elegant curves of the petals and the textured surface of the pot. The simplicity of the subject matter is elevated by the artist's attention to detail, inviting viewers to appreciate the quiet beauty found in the ordinary.
Canvas Size: 10*12 (25*30 cm)
