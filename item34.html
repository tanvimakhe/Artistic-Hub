Sunset Island

Rs. 3500

Category: Landscape Painting

The painting depicts a beautiful sunset over a tropical island. The foreground is filled with lush, green palm trees, their silhouettes standing out against the colorful sky. The water in the midground is a shimmering turquoise, reflecting the hues of the sunset above. In the background, the island's coastline can be seen, with its white sand beaches and rocky cliffs. The sunset itself is the focal point of the painting. The sky is ablaze with color, from the fiery reds and oranges of the sun to the pinks and purples of the clouds. The sun is just beginning to sink below the horizon, casting a golden glow over the entire scene.
Canvas Size 8*10 (20*25cm)
