Twilight Harmony

Rs. 6500

Category: Landscape Painting

"Twilight Harmony" is a mesmerizing painting that captures the serene beauty of an evening landscape. The canvas is awash with vibrant hues of orange and purple, reflecting the tranquil transition from day to night. A calm lake mirrors the rich colors of the sky, creating a harmonious blend. Small flowers in the foreground add a delicate touch, their vivid colors echoing the brilliance of the setting sun. The painting invites viewers into a peaceful realm where the beauty of nature unfolds in a symphony of colors and quietude.
Canvas Size 12*18 (30*45 cm)
