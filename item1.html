Serenity's Blessing

Rs.3500

Category: Acrylic Painting

"Serenity's Blessing " is a captivating abstract painting that captures the tranquil essence of Lord Ganesha in a moment of serene repose. In this artwork, Lord Ganesha is depicted in a seated posture, radiating calmness and bestowing blessings upon the beholder. The colour palette chosen for this piece is a soothing blend of azure blues and soft, earthy greens, creating an atmosphere of peace and serenity. Lord Ganesha is represented as an abstract, ethereal figure, his form emerging from the interplay of gentle brushstrokes and flowing lines. The modak, held gracefully in one hand, is symbolically portrayed as a source of divine sweetness and abundance.
Canvas Size: 12*18 (30*45 cm)
