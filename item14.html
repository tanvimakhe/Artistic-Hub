Silhouette Serenity

Rs. 3000

Category: Acrylic Painting

"Silhouette Serenity" paints a breathtaking sunset over a tranquil river, casting a warm, golden glow across the water. The focal point is a majestic dried tree standing as a silhouette against the vibrant hues of the setting sun. The reflection of the sky on the river adds an ethereal touch, capturing the quiet beauty of nature during this serene twilight moment. The artwork invites viewers to embrace the peaceful ambiance of the scene, where the beauty of the setting sun and the grace of the tree create a harmonious dance between light and shadow.
Canvas Size 14*16 (30*40cm)
