Elegance in Profile: Aishwarya Rai as Jodha

Rs. 3500

Category: Sketch Art

This sketch captures the timeless beauty of Aishwarya Rai in her iconic Jodha look. The focus is on her side profile, highlighting the graceful contours of her face and the intricate details of the Jodha-inspired ensemble. The artist skillfully renders Aishwarya's expressive eyes and the regal aura associated with her portrayal of Jodha Bai. "Elegance in Profile" pays homage to the actress's captivating beauty in a role that left a lasting imprint on cinematic and artistic memory.
