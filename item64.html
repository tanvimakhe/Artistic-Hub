Peacock Mandala

Rs. 5000

Category: Mandala Art

Peacock Mandala is a stunning blend of intricate geometry and the vibrant beauty of peacock feathers. This captivating art form features a central mandala design adorned with delicate patterns and symmetry, mirroring the graceful and elaborate plumage of a peacock. The radiant hues of blues, greens, and golds create a visual spectacle, while the mandala's symmetrical structure adds a sense of harmony and balance. The peacock, symbolizing beauty and elegance, serves as a focal point within the mandala, its feathers gracefully integrated into the mesmerizing patterns. This unique combination results in a symbolic and visually arresting representation that celebrates the regal allure of both the peacock and the ancient mandala art form.
