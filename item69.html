Melodies of Divinity

Rs. 3000

Category: Sketch Art

This sketch portrays Lord Krishna in a moment of divine serenity, adorned in a beautiful pagdi and holding his enchanting flute. The artist captures the graceful curves of Lord Krishna's form, and the intricate details of the pagdi add a regal touch. With a contemplative expression, Krishna seems immersed in the ethereal melodies emanating from his flute. "Melodies of Divinity" not only pays homage to the artistic richness of Krishna's portrayal but also evokes a sense of spiritual harmony and the eternal beauty associated with the Hindu deity.
