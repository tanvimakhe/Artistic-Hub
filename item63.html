Lippan art (Mandala)

Rs. 7000

Category: Mandala Art

Lippan art mandala is a traditional form of mural painting originating from the Kutch region of Gujarat, India. This unique art form involves intricate geometric designs and mirror inlays created with a mixture of clay and camel dung. The designs are meticulously handcrafted on walls, ceilings, or other surfaces, forming mesmerizing mandalas that reflect the cultural richness of the region. The mirrors embedded in the clay catch and reflect light, creating a sparkling effect. Lippan art mandalas often depict cultural motifs, folklore, or religious symbols, showcasing a harmonious blend of artistic expression and traditional craftsmanship. These vibrant and reflective artworks not only serve as decorative elements but also symbolize the community's resilience and connection to their heritage.
