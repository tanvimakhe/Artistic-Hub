Regal Resplendence

Rs.3500

Category: Acrylic Painting

"Regal Resplendence" portrays an Indian queen in all her regal glory, adorned in opulent attire and seated against a richly detailed traditional background. The intricately carved frame surrounding the painting echoes the grandeur of royal architecture, enhancing the overall majestic ambiance. The queen, draped in vibrant silks and adorned with intricate jewelry, exudes grace and authority. The background is a tapestry of traditional patterns, reflecting the cultural richness of India. "Regal Resplendence" captures not just the queen's physical beauty, but also the cultural heritage and enduring elegance that define her royal presence.
Canvas Size: 10*12 (25*30 cm)
